Street Bike
  • Yamaha MT 15

Yamaha MT 15 is available at a price range of Rs. 1.40 Lakh to 1.41 Lakh in India. It is available in 3 variants and 3 colours. The MT 15 is a powered by 155cc BS6 engine mated to a 6 is speed gearbox . This engine of MT 15 develops a power of 18.5 ps and a torque of 13.9 nm .

Cruiser Bike
  • Royal Enfield Classic 350cc

The Royal Enfield Classic 350 is priced from Rs 1,72,466 for the single-channel ABS version and from Rs 1,80,877 for the dual-channel variant (both ex-showroom Delhi). On certain colours of the dual-channel ABS trim, you also get alloy rims.

Commoter Bike
  • Bajaj platinum

Bajaj Platina is a 100cc motorcycle manufactured by Bajaj Auto. The Platina was launched in April 2006 at an ex-showroom price of Rs 35,000 and crossed sales of 500,000 units within eight months of its launch.

Scooter Bike

The very popular Honda Activa 5G received a major update at the Auto Expo 2018 in February, and now the company has listed the scooter on its website at a starting price of Rs 52,460 (ex-showroom) for the standard variant and Rs 54,325 (ex-showroom) for the Deluxe variant.
